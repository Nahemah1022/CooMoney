Empowering Financial Management for Organizations

Explore the Future of Finance with CooMoney

CooMoney presents an advanced web-based financing application tailored to the dynamic needs of modern large-scale organizations. Our platform redefines how finances are managed, offering a comprehensive solution for overseeing and analyzing diverse financial activities while harnessing the potential of shared funding sources.

Key Features at a Glance

Experience the Power of CooMoney's Features

  • Expense Tracking: Seamlessly track expenses across various projects and initiatives, gaining real-time insights into spending patterns.
  • Budget Management: Set, monitor, and optimize budgets for individual projects or across the organization, ensuring efficient resource allocation.
  • Financial Reporting: Generate insightful reports that provide a clear overview of your organization's financial health, aiding informed decision-making.
  • Multi-Project Integration: Simultaneously manage financial aspects of multiple ongoing projects under a unified platform, fostering collaboration and synergy.

Technologies Behind CooMoney

CooMoney is powered by a robust technology stack that ensures seamless functionality and performance:

  • Front-End: Built using Vue.js, providing a responsive and interactive user interface.
  • Back-End: Leveraging the capabilities of Node.js to handle data processing and server-side logic.
  • Database: MongoDB serves as the reliable and efficient data store for CooMoney's operations.
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS EC2 instances for scalability and reliability.
  • Storage: AWS S3 is used for secure and scalable storage of files and media.
